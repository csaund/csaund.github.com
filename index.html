About Me
I will receive my B.S. in Computer Science and Cognitive and Brain Sciences from Tufts University in May 2015. My main focus in both areas of study has primarily been Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, which I intend to continue in graduate studies.
I am motivated by passion. Whatever the task, being surrounded by passionate people brings out my most inspired and creative work. I love working in teams small enough to personally interact with each member every day, and take as easily to leading a project I care about as I do to following a team lead's vision.
I have also been described as an avid world traveller. For more about who I am and how I fill my time, check out what I do and where I've been up above.

Professional Goals
I am searching for a company that speaks to my ideal increase-awesome, decrease-suck world balance. Which is to say, I want to work somewhere that cares about its global positive impact. Whether it's tools that enable others to be more effective, or a direct channel for charitable influence, I care deeply about improving the world around me.
I hope to attend a PhD program in Machine Learning within the next 5 years.
